About OASIS

The OASIS Family of Contract Vehicles

  • Specifically designed for the acquisition of professional services
  • Enables the use of ANY contract type
  • Separated into unrestricted (OASIS) and Small Business (OASIS SB) to make it easy to reserve task orders for small business categories
  • All vendors have DCAA-vetted financial systems

What are Pools?

  • OASIS spans seven major functional categories that are referred to as pools.

SAM

last updated
We pull SAM data directly from the System for Award Management (SAM) to provide supplemental information on all of the vendors in OASIS, including their SAM registration date, any potential suspensions or debarments, and the NAICS codes that a vendor may contract under.

FPDS

last updated
Discovery loads data from the Federal Procurement Data System (FPDS) to automatically provide you with a context-rich contract history for each OASIS vendor, including the amount of the contract, the primary NAICS code and the pricing type.
