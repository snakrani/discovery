Building Maintenance and Operations

Building Maintenance and Operations (BMO) strategic sourcing solution is a comprehensive and flexible solution covering all high-demand BMO services. It is an open market, multiple-award, indefinite delivery, indefinite quantity (MA-IDIQ), government-wide contract vehicle supporting the strategic sourcing initiative to reduce costs and drive efficient purchasing by federal agencies.

  • Best-in-Class
  • Provides a broad and comprehensive range of BMO services for use throughout
  • Provides a regionally focused pool of highly qualified large and small contractors
  • Allows flexibility for contract types, including fixed price, labor hour, time and materials, and hybrids
  • Leverages spend in acquiring services in an effective, economical, and expedient manner resulting in both significant cost and time savings
  • Following FAR Subpart 16.505 Ordering Procedures, all BMO vendors that can meet the exact specifications of your requirement should be notified (given Fair Opportunity to compete)
