HcaTS

Human Capital and Training Solutions

The HCaTS Program is the result of partnership between Office of Personnel Management (OPM) and General Services Administration (GSA). HCaTS is comprised of two indefinite-delivery indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) governmentwide contract vehicles: HCaTS and HCaTS SB. These vehicles provide reliable, flexible, fast and efficient ways to obtain the best value customized solutions for human capital management and training requirements.

  • Best-in-Class
  • Provides customized Training and Development, Human Capital Strategy, and Organizational Performance Improvement Services
  • Highest Technically Rated with Fair and Reasonable (HTR-FRP) Contractors that have been pre-screened by HC specialists
  • Leveraged Category and Performance Management Strength
  • Allows ancillary services & other direct costs at the task order level
